What do you want to know about us?

“The hardest skill to acquire in this sport is the one where you compete all out, give it all you have, and you are still getting beat no matter what you do. When you have the killer instinct to fight through that, it is very special.”

It all starts with you providing us with a blueprint of your clothing idea for us to study. After discussion on your tech packs, we develop a prototype of your clothing design.

Bulk production is the final step inside our clothing factory post pre-production samples approval.

We use the exact grade and quality of raw materials that you pay for and we commit to. As a high quality clothing manufacturer & exporter ourselves we don’t require sourcing agents or meditators. This avoids miscommunication and provides transparency.
